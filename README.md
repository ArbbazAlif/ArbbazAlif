# 💫 About Me:
I started my journey in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, where I built a strong foundation in problem-solving and system thinking. Over time, I became increasingly curious about how technology connects with business decisions, which led me to explore courses in business strategy and management. An early internship in design and development engineering further shaped my perspective—it showed me how real-world problems require not just technical solutions, but thoughtful, user-focused approaches. That experience ultimately inspired me to pursue a Master’s in Business Analytics and AI at UT Dallas.<br><br>Throughout my master’s, I’ve worked at the intersection of data, AI, and product—building solutions, participating in competitions, and earning recognition along the way. These experiences have strengthened my ability to turn data into meaningful insights and drive impact through AI-driven systems. As I approach graduation in May 2026, I’m actively seeking full-time opportunities in data analytics, data science, and AI product domains, where I can continue building solutions that create real-world value.
